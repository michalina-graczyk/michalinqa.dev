Preparation for job interviews for QA and testing positions. I will help you present your experience and competencies in a convincing way.

## For whom?

- QA Engineers and testers applying for new positions
- Testers preparing for their first interview for a QA Lead position
- People changing industries or returning to the job market
- Candidates for QA Engineer and QA Lead positions

## What will you gain?

- **Feedback** on your resume and LinkedIn profile
- **Mock interview** with questions typical for your position
- **Tips** on how to talk about your experience
- **Preparation** for behavioral and situational questions

## How does it look?

A preparation session usually lasts 60-90 minutes. We start by analyzing the job offer and your experience, then we go through a mock interview with constructive feedback.
