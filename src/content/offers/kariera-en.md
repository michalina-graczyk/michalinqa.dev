Help in planning and executing your career path in IT - whether you want to develop as a technical expert or transition to a leadership role.

## For whom?

- IT specialists considering their next career step
- People considering transitioning from a technical role to management
- Tech Leads and managers looking for new challenges
- People returning to work after a break (e.g., parental leave)

## What will you gain?

- **Clarity** about your goals and development opportunities
- **Competency map** - what you already have and what is worth working on
- **Action plan** with concrete steps
- **Perspective** of someone who has walked a similar path

## How does it look?

We start with a conversation about your current situation, experience, and goals. Then we jointly analyze possible development paths and create an action plan tailored to your needs and possibilities.
