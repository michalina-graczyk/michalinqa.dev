Individual consultations for IT specialists and team leaders who want to develop their skills and achieve better results.

## For whom?

- QA Engineers and Test Automation Specialists planning career development
- Tech Leads and Engineering Managers facing leadership challenges
- IT professionals seeking support in developing soft skills
- People building a quality culture in their organizations

## What will you gain?

- **A fresh perspective** on your professional challenges
- **Concrete tips** based on practical experience
- **Action plan** tailored to your situation
- **Support** in making difficult decisions

## How does it look?

Meetings are held online (Google Meet or Zoom). We adjust the duration and frequency to your needs - from a one-time consultation to regular mentoring sessions.
